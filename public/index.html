About the (NPO)

Learn More about Moletsane Foundation (NPO)

When it was started

Moletsane Foundation (NPO) was incorporated in South Africa December 2020 under registration as an Non-Profit-Organisation.

Company Vision

To especially address malnutrition, hunger and play a leading role in empowering communities with skills that lead to self-reliance and independency.

Company Mission

To uplift disadvantaged communities with particular focus on childrens health, growth, development and wellbeing.
